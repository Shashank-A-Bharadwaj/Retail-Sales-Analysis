# Retail Sales Analysis
## Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing a retail company’s sales dataset to uncover **high-performing product categories, profitable regions, and monthly sales trends**.  
The objective is to convert raw transactional data into **actionable business insights** that help improve profitability and strategic decision-making.


### Objective
- Identify top-selling product categories  
- Analyze region-wise revenue and profit contribution  
- Understand monthly sales and profit trends  
- Provide data-driven business recommendations  


### Tools Used
- Python
- Pandas
- Matplotlib
- Seaborn

### Key Insights
## 📈 Analysis Performed

### Top-Selling Categories
- Aggregated sales data to identify categories contributing the highest revenue.

### Regional Performance
- Compared sales and profit across regions to determine top-performing markets.

### Monthly Sales & Profit Trends
- Analyzed month-wise sales and profit to detect seasonal demand patterns.

### Region vs Category Analysis
- Used heatmaps and bar charts to evaluate category performance across different regions.

### Business Recommendations
- Focus marketing and inventory on top-performing categories  
- Strengthen presence in high-revenue regions  
- Introduce promotions during low-performing months  
- Implement region-specific strategies based on category demand
